Server Features

MapleServer will take you to a great adventure with our unique systems accompanying you through your grind. The list below shows some of our selected features.

Low Rates

With 2/1/1 server rates, MapleServer provides an exciting and challenging twist to keep the adventure going!

No HP Washing

Tired of creating a Mage just to be able to play your desired class? Good! Try MapleServer today and play your favorite class right away!

Live Restarts

No more old-fashioned server restarts. MapleServer's unique live restarts system won't require you to logout when there is a server restart!

Party Quests

All working up to v0.62 GMS-like party quests for your enjoyment, and maybe more coming your way soon!

Anti-leech System

MapleServer discourages players from buying leech from other players to encourage party-play.

Updated Cosmetics

Although we are a nostalgic server, our Stylist NPCs will provide you with updated cosmetics from higher GMS versions.

Skill Balancing

We are constantly working to make all classes viable, relevant and enjoyable as possible by applying our own unique changes to certain skills and mechanics.

Social & Trading Utilities

MapleServer has systems in place to ease your search for new friends, trades, grinding partners, guilds and more.
